translation english

1
To whom shall I dedicate this charming little book,
just now polished with dry pumice stone?
To you, Cornelius; for you valued
these trifles,
ever since you dared, alone among the Italians,
to unfold the entire course of history in three volumes,
learned, by Jupiter, and full of effort.
So, take this little book, whatever its worth may be;
and may it, O Virgin, my patroness,
live on, ever youthful, beyond a century!
